Demo is Live!
August 14th, 2022

Hello!

I finally have a demo of my game, ready to be played in your browser right now! Just click the image below here, and you'll be taken to a link where you can give it a try. Hope you enjoy it, and feel free to provide any feedback you might have. 😁

Shinobi SunClan Demo
2D Game
Demo for my longterm 2D RPG, which focuses on combat in randomly-generated dungeons.

You can also access this demo from the "Portfolio" page of this site at any time. This is the first time I tried creating a WebGL build, and I think it's a much, much better way to put games out there for people to see. I am going to spend some time making WebGL versions of all my other games as well, so they're more easily accessible.

As always, I will continue to work on this game to make new features and squash bugs. Stay tuned as well for a new side project I'm working on as well; hope you like fishing! 🎣
