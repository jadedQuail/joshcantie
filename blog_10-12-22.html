Fishing Village is Complete!
October 12th, 2022

Hello!

Very happy to say that I am finished with my fishing game, which is called Fishing Village. The game is as the title advertises, it's a village in which you fish. Have a ball! See if you can catch every species.

In terms of personal growth, I explored a few new concepts with this game, one of which was implementing a little bit of physics, which you can see when you catch a fish (it flies out at the player as a projectile with an arc). I also got to play more with probabilities and spawn rates, and I especially firmed up my grasp on how weighted probabilities can be implemented in Unity. The thing I'm most proud of with this game, however, is that I made it in only 2 months, and my scripting definitely stepped up in terms of efficiency. It's really satisfying to be able to put together a fun little polished game so quickly!
