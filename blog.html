My First Post
June 23rd, 2020

Hello, my name is Josh and this is my new blog!

Tonight is the first night that this site goes live. I've decided to create this blog and website to both keep track of the various tech projects I do and to give myself something to look back at in the future. I just graduated from the University of Notre Dame this May - four years that felt like 30 seconds! - and now I have some time on my hands to create, play, and hang out with friends.

Unfortunately, I no longer have a cheap and easy way to use Autodesk Maya for modeling and animation now that my student license is no longer effective; I love Maya but I don't think I can stomach the cost right now! So it's looking like for a start I will be familiarizing myself with Blender for this summer. Hopefully I can create some assets to use in a new Unity project that I've been brainstorming, but more on that in the future.

--Josh
